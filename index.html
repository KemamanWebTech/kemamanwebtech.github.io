Who are we?

Kemaman Web Tech is a small tech company based in Cyberjaya. We work on websites and applications for small businesses and personnel with tight budgets and we love doing it. We've been around since 2016. Our goal is to provide satisfactory solutions to our clients which don't break their budget. If you have a project with small budget, don't sweat, you can reach us.

How can we help you?

The internet today is filled with far too many template sites which usually unable to tell the whole story or to convey truthfully what your business has. It's just too bland and makes little efforts to truly set you apart from the others. That's where our passion comes in. We want to set you apart. Put your thoughts on paper and we are ready to go.
WordPress is an open source platform optimized for blog-like websites. If you often post articles or news on your website then Wordpress is a very favorable solution. WordPress is quick and easy to install and gives you a lot of control over the content on your website.
Be it mobile or web applications, we also work on developing small-to-medium projects such as Warehouse Inventory Management System, HR system, Ticketing system etc. Reach out to us with your requirements and we'll have a go at it.
We also offer website hosting so you don't have to go through the hassle of getting the site working with the other hosting providers. If you're looking for a web hosting without having us to build the website for you, you should ask us too.
We also provide email hosting to our clients. Email hosting will now be included by default in all of our hosting plans. It'll help set your business apart with a custom your-name@business-name email address. If you just need the email, give us a call too.
We also provide cheap VPS if you need one for your temporary or long term projects. You will get complete root access to your server as if it were your dedicated server. You will have complete control over your server and almost the same benefits of the pricey dedicated server.
